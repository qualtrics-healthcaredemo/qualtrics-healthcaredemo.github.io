About Dr. Felix Cullen

About the provider

Felix J. Cullen, MD, is a dedicated cardiologist at Tread Health's Southwest Cardiology clinic in St. George. He supports patients in St. George and surrounding communities, focusing on general cardiology, metabolic health, and obesity management. He believes in empowering patients by providing clear information about their conditions and the benefits and risks of various treatments, allowing them to make informed decisions about their health. Dr. Cullen emphasizes the importance of proper nutrition and regular exercise as essential components for overall health and longevity.

Dr. Cullen is committed to giving honest answers based on his knowledge and experience. He follows national guidelines for treatment recommendations and only suggests tests when they will impact the treatment plan. Fluent in Spanish, he ensures that language is never a barrier to quality care.

Outside work, the Preston, Idaho native enjoys riding his bike and spending time outdoors with his wife and their two dogs. A lifelong learner, he loves reading and is always open to book recommendations or exploring new places. Dr. Cullen and his wife have been married for over 30 years and are proud parents of three adult children.
