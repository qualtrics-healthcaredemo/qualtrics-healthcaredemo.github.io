Understanding Statements & Coverage

  • 4 Dec 2019
  • 12 Comments
  • Health Tips

You can check the status of your claims by starting on the claims list. We label claims that have been denied or need additional information, so they're easy to find on the list. We also include status information when you click into each claim.Claims may not appear on the site immediately after you receive care. It does take some time for us to process details from your provider.

Our goal is to help you get the proper care for your condition, but there are some types of care that we do not pay for. We review the services your plan covers and nationally recognized guidelines and policies before denying a claim.

If you have checked what's covered by your plan and still have questions about why a claim was denied, you can call us at Member Services or file an appeal.
